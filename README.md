# Privacy Policy — IELTS Speaking Prep Coach

_Last updated: 2026-07-08_

IELTS Speaking Prep Coach ("we", "the app") is an independent study app
operated by Shi Xiaohong. This policy explains what data the app collects,
why, and your choices.

**Non-affiliation.** IELTS Speaking Prep Coach is not affiliated with,
endorsed by, or connected to British Council, IDP: IELTS Australia, or
Cambridge Assessment English. Feedback and estimated bands produced by the
app are for practice guidance only and are not official IELTS results.

## 1. Data we collect

- **Account data.** When you sign in, we store your email address and a
  user ID via Supabase (our authentication and database provider).
- **Practice content.** Your recorded answers, transcripts, and AI-generated
  feedback are stored in your account so you can review them later.
- **Purchase records.** If you buy a subscription or one-off credit through
  Apple In-App Purchase, we receive a transaction identifier and entitlement
  status from Apple. We do **not** receive your credit card number.
- **Microphone audio.** Recording is triggered only when you tap the record
  button. Audio is sent to our server for transcription and AI scoring, then
  stored with your practice history.
- **Basic device info.** App version, OS version, and anonymous error logs
  for debugging.

We do **not** use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and we do
**not** track you across other apps or websites. The app's iOS privacy
manifest declares `NSPrivacyTracking = false`.

## 2. How we use data

- Provide AI feedback on your speaking answers.
- Save your practice history so you can review past sessions.
- Verify and restore your paid entitlements.
- Fix bugs and improve the app.

We do **not** sell your data, and we do **not** share it with advertisers.

## 3. Third-party services

- **Supabase** — authentication and database hosting.
- **Apple In-App Purchase** — payment processing.
- **AI model provider(s)** — your transcript is sent to a large language
  model provider (e.g. OpenAI / Anthropic) solely to generate feedback. We
  do not authorize these providers to train models on your data.

## 4. Data retention & deletion

Your practice history stays in your account until you delete it or delete
your account. To delete your account and all associated data, email
**xh.shi0507@gmail.com** with the subject "Delete my account". We will
process the request within 30 days.

## 5. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has
created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

## 6. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the
current version. Material changes will be announced inside the app.
