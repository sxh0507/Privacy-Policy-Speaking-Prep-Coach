# Privacy Policy — Speaking Prep Coach

_Last updated: 2026-07-08_

IELTS Speaking Prep Coach ("we", "the app") is an independent study app
operated by Shi Xiaohong. This policy explains what data the app collects,
where it lives, and your choices.

**Non-affiliation.** IELTS Speaking Prep Coach is not affiliated with,
endorsed by, or connected to British Council, IDP: IELTS Australia, or
Cambridge Assessment English. Feedback and estimated bands produced by the
app are for practice guidance only and are not official IELTS results.

## 1. Data we collect and where it lives

**Stored on your device only** (in the app's local storage, never uploaded
to our servers):

- **Recordings.** Audio you record while practising, saved to on-device
  storage so you can replay it. Recording is triggered only when you tap
  the record button.
- **Transcripts and AI feedback.** The text version of your answer and the
  feedback returned by our AI service.
- **Practice history.** Mock exam reports, session progress, daily quota
  counters, and your in-app preferences.

If you delete the app, all of the above is deleted with it.

**Stored on our server (Supabase)** — the minimum needed to run your
account:

- **Account.** Email address and a Supabase-generated user ID.
- **Purchase records.** Apple In-App Purchase transaction identifiers and
  entitlement status (whether you have an active subscription or remaining
  credits). We never receive your credit card number.
- **Question bank.** The IELTS-style practice questions themselves are
  hosted on Supabase. This is public content, not your data.

**Sent temporarily to third-party AI providers** (processed on demand and
not retained by us):

- **Audio.** Your recording is sent through our server to OpenAI Whisper
  for speech-to-text, then discarded on our side.
- **Transcript + question text.** Sent to OpenAI's GPT models to generate
  feedback, then discarded on our side. Per OpenAI's API terms, API inputs
  are not used to train their models by default. Audio and transcripts are
  processed on OpenAI's servers, which are located in the United States.

**Basic device info.** App version, OS version, and anonymous error logs
for debugging.

We do **not** use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and we do
**not** track you across other apps or websites. The app's iOS privacy
manifest declares `NSPrivacyTracking = false`.

## 2. How we use data

- Provide AI feedback on your speaking answers.
- Keep your practice history available on your device so you can review
  past sessions.
- Verify and restore your paid entitlements.
- Fix bugs and improve the app.

We do **not** sell your data, and we do **not** share it with advertisers.

## 3. Third-party services

- **Supabase** — hosts our authentication, purchase records, and question
  bank. Your practice recordings, transcripts, and feedback are **not**
  stored on Supabase.
- **OpenAI** — transcribes your audio (Whisper) and generates feedback
  (GPT). Audio and transcripts are processed transiently. OpenAI's own
  data-handling policy applies; per OpenAI's API terms, API inputs are not
  used to train their models by default. Processing takes place on
  OpenAI's servers in the United States.
- **Apple In-App Purchase** — processes payments. We receive only
  transaction identifiers, not payment details.

## 4. Data retention & deletion

- **On-device data** (recordings, transcripts, feedback, history) is
  deleted the moment you uninstall the app or clear its storage in
  iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → IELTS Speaking Prep Coach →
  Offload / Delete.
- **Server-side data** (your email, user ID, and purchase records on
  Supabase) is kept until you request deletion. To delete your account and
  all server-side data, email **xh.shi0507@gmail.com** with the subject
  "Delete my account". We will process the request within 30 days.

## 5. Data security

- All traffic between the app and our server is encrypted with HTTPS.
- Account and purchase records on Supabase are stored in encrypted
  databases and accessed only through authenticated requests.
- Recordings and transcripts never leave your device except transiently
  for AI processing as described in Section 1.

## 6. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has
created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

## 7. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the
current version. Material changes will be announced inside the app.
